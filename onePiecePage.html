Top 3 Strongest Characters in the One Piece Universe

Back

Monkey D. Luffy

Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hats with incredible strength, speed, and durability. After mastering Gear 5, his rubber body becomes almost limitless, allowing him to fight on equal terms with the strongest beings in the world.

Gol D. Roger

Roger was the Pirate King, known for his unmatched Haki and combat abilities. His willpower and strength were so great that he reached the final island and discovered the secrets of the world.

White Beard

Whitebeard was known as the “Strongest Man in the World.” His Quake-Quake power could destroy islands, and his physical strength and Haki made him feared by the entire navy.
