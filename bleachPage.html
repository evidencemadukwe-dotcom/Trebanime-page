Top 3 Strongest Characters in the Bleach Universe

Back

Ichigo Kurosaki

Ichigo is a teenager who becomes a Soul Reaper and gains powers from many sources — Shinigami, Hollow, Quincy, and Fullbring. He fights to protect his friends and becomes one of the strongest beings in all the realms.

Yhwach

Yhwach is the king of the Quincies and can give and take powers. With The Almighty, he can see and change the future, making him almost impossible to defeat. His goal is to reshape the world to his own vision.

Genryūsai Yamamoto

Yamamoto is the Captain-Commander of the Gotei 13, feared for his overwhelming fire abilities. His Bankai burns hotter than the sun, and few characters can survive his full power.
